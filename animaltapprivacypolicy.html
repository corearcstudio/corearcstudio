Privacy Policy for Tap the Animal

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction

We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. Tap the Animal ("the App") is designed to be a safe, educational, and fun environment for users of all ages, especially children. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users.

  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account or login to use the App.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services to track user behavior.
  • No Location Data: We do not request or access your geographic location.

3. Local Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device:

  • Game Data: Your high scores, accumulated stars, unlocked animals, and shop items are saved directly to your device's storage (UserDefaults on iOS / localStorage on Web).
  • Settings: Your preferences for sound and haptic feedback are stored locally.
  • Deletion: If you delete the App from your device or clear your browser cache, this data is permanently removed. We cannot restore it because we do not have a copy.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers a "Premium Upgrade" via In-App Purchase (IAP).

  • Payment Processing: All financial transactions are handled exclusively by Apple (via the App Store) or the respective platform provider.
  • Data Access: We never see, collect, or store your credit card information, billing address, or transaction history. We only receive a "receipt" from the App Store indicating whether the purchase was successful so we can unlock the features.
